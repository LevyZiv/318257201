About us...

Fashion Responsible is a platform for selling & buying hand me down clothes.
We believe that fashion should be available to everyone, with minimum pollution and prices, and think that hand me down clothes are the best way to achieve that.
Our website offers you the possibility to buy & sell hand me down clothes from the comfort of your own home.
Here in FR we are a community, and are always available for our users at: FashionResponsible@gmail.com
